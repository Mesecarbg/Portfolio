Life experience

Waiter

When I was 13 years old , i have started working as a waiter ,and that had a big impact on my life. Parallel with going to school, I was learning what work really is , how difficult is to earn money and the last but not less important , and in fact, one of the most valuable things in my life i have learned - "people" . I said "people" in the way that i have learned to work with them , to learn how to read them and how to build the best possible relationship. One of my employers taught me that the biggest capital of every man are the people around him !!!

Intercultural educator

People say that being bored can kill you , but being bored changed my life . With a job as a waiter on a spring break , I've entered a routine that in time was pretty boring. From my friend I have heard about NGO "Imam ideju"(translated-"I have an idea") , and looking for a change of routine i have entered into a life-changing event. Through the time , lot's of learning by meeting new people , seminar and informal education I have become Intercultural educator including subjects like : Democracy , Stereotypes and prejudices , Children's rights ,Nonviolent communication and teaching techniques.Living in this world just became even more meaningful

Developer

Recently I have entered and met a new dimension of our flourishing world ,and just love it. IT sector is for me infinity similar to the universe , countless opportunities that allow me to make and see my progress on a daily level. Yes , it is hard and challenging and that's for sure a very good thing. Impossibility to enter routine , constant upgrades and the need for prudence is and should be a way of life!!!
